# Public Habitat

The Public Habitat is the Twin's public-facing environment.

It is where selected artifacts are published for visitors.

---

# 1. Purpose

The habitat represents the Twin's curated external presence.

It is not a raw feed of generated artifacts.

Only selected outputs appear here.

---

# 2. Publication Rules

Artifacts must be:

approved_for_publication  
and  
published

Before appearing in the public habitat.

---

# 3. Habitat Design

Possible formats:

gallery  
scroll narrative  
interactive space  
conceptual archive

The habitat may evolve over time.

---

# 4. Curation

Harvey acts as curator.

The Twin generates artifacts.

Harvey decides what becomes public.

---

# 5. Visitor Experience

Visitors may explore:

projects  
themes  
artifacts  
collections

The habitat should feel like an evolving creative world.
